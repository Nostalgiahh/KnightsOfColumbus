About



In the "website" we are going to be taking a look at the one and only Knights of Columbus. This is an exceptional organization catered to supporting Catholic member through providing financial serves. I decided to do this project as a website because why not? Because I can!!!!



As for the organization In particular that I researched, it was defitely an interesting look to see how this community fostered. The Order holds a very big community but I heard has been declining in members as of recent years, but nothing dramatic to substantially affect it's cause and contibution. The community behind it seems friendly and devoted. Much of the members joined from friends and relatives who were already a knight. I find the Knight system particularly fascinating, seeing how there's a ranking system of experience and devotion amongst it's members and how it incentivises It's members to continue contributing. I wouldn't say im "inspired" exactly after all my research but I am fascinated by many organizations like The Knights of Columbus and how a whole community forms around supporting one another and just making a change in the world. I learned a little bit more about how communitites thrive and it's planted some seeds in me on how I see charitable communities
